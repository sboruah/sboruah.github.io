I belong to a tiny corner of the country, India. I have always been an admirer of music, art & adventure. I wanted to do several things when I was a child, ranging from babysitting to paleontology. Some people say I could never grow up, so I decided to be a designer. I believe design sense (research + understanding + development + iterations) in us develops right from our childhood (we ask questions + cook up stories + make finger puppets + solve puzzles). Besides photography, making illustrations and exploring various materials to work with, I am fond of playing the guitar and the bongo, scribbling in my notebook, star gazing, feeding street cats and observing old people to wonder how they must’ve looked like when they were young. Lights and machines fascinate me. I love to make doodles and eat fried noodles.
